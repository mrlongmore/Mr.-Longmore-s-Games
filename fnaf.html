This game contains mild horror elements inspired by Five Nights at Freddy's. The animatronics will cause a game over if they catch you, but there are no graphic images.

How to Play:

  • Watch both doors for approaching animatronics
  • Click the light buttons to toggle lights on/off (costs 1 battery token every 2 seconds)
  • Monsters are completely invisible unless you shine a light on them
  • Monsters become visible with red warning borders in the final 2 seconds before attacking
  • A red timer bar will appear when a monster is present
  • Click the door buttons to close doors when you see a monster
  • You have 5 seconds to close the door or you'll get jumpscared
  • Each door closure uses one battery token and lasts 5 seconds
  • Closing a door will scare away any monster and prevent new ones for 2 seconds
  • Collect battery tokens by practicing ASL vocabulary:
    • VERBS: Click ONCE (OPEN-BOOK, CLOSE-BOOK, etc.)
    • NOUNS: Click TWICE (BOOK, CHAIR, WINDOW, etc.)
  • You have 1.5 seconds to click the correct number of times
  • Wrong clicks will cause the card to shake and turn red
